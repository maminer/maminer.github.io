Dr. Miner is Social Scientist and Research Methodologist focused on the interplay of inequality and institutions.

He received his Ph.D. in Sociology from the University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee where he studied school segregation, public perceptions and research methodology. He also holds a M.A. in Sociology from the University of Wisconsin, Milwaukee and a B.A. in Psychology and Sociology from St. Joseph's College, Brooklyn. He specializes in quantitative methods, education, social media and technology.
Dr. Miner is currently off the job market. His publications and professional experience can be found on his CV.
